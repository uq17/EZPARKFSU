Your Account and Rewards

Help us make it easier for you to find open spots while earning rewards points you can spend! View your EZ-Park Rewards below or click any of the areas below to earn points!

My Rewards Points:

839

Earn Rewards:

Work towards a Super Spot - Click the button to share your location and earn points if you park at any of our designated locations around the outer edge of campus.


Log your alternative transportation miles - Click the button to start sharing your location and we'll give you rewards points based on how long you haven't parked a vehicle in any FSU spot. Points will only be rewarded if your location is connected to EZ-Park each full business day.


Let us know if you are leaving your spot - Click the button to start sharing your location before you get to your spot and we'll give you the reward points when we confirm you've left.


Report an incident - If you see a closure, incident, or blockage at a parking location.


Spend Rewards:

Super Spot - Click the button to apply for a Super Spot, which we have reserved for users to purchase with their EZ-Park points in the central parking garages of Traditions and Woodward and park in for up to a week.


Transfer to Spear-It Rewards - Click the button to transfer your EZ-Park Rewards points to Spear-It Rewards points instead.
