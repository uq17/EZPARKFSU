ABOUT

EZ-Park FSU website is a mobile-responsive website for students, faculty, and staff at FSU to view parking statistics and plan campus visits without the hassle. We want to make it easier for you to be able find a parking spot on campus where you want and when you want, so you can enjoy your campus experience on your own time.

* Note: this website is for presentation purposes only and parking data is not accurately represented. *

Availability

Visit the Availability page to view info about parking garages and lots at FSU, including how many spots are currently available, directions, and any closures.

Parking Map

Visit the Parking Map page to get a better idea of where spots are located at FSU, where users who are leaving their spots are located, and any other notifications.

Plan a Trip

Visit the Plan a Trip page before driving to campus to find the most optimal place to park on campus based on the preferences you select.


Notifications

Visit the Notifications page to view current notifications regarding parking garages and lots and report any closures or events you may encounter.

Account

Visit your Account page to view your EZ-Park Rewards points, which you can earn to help us make it easier to find you parking and spend on exclusive rewards.
